ONLY ONE ARTIST WILL RISE

DRAW, VOTE, AND PROVE YOUR DOMINANCE

In this real-time multiplayer drawing game, you and up to 7 friends compete for glory. A random player picks the topic, everyone draws at the same time, and the group votes on who nailed it. Fast matches, brutal honesty, one winner.

Built On

React

Builds the entire game interface with a custom screen-based router — no React Router needed. Game state flows through all screens via props.

Node.js

Powers the backend with an event-loop architecture, handling thousands of simultaneous player connections without spawning a single extra thread.

Express.js

Manages server routes and the Socket.io integration, keeping communication between the frontend and backend organized and fast.

MongoDB

Stores completed game sessions, player scores, and match history — accessible from the Matches screen after every game.

How It Works

01. Create or Join

The host generates a 6-character room code. Other players join instantly by entering it along with their name — no account or login required.

02. Draw Together

A random player picks the topic. Everyone draws simultaneously on their own canvas

03. Vote & Win

After the timer, each canvas is shown for 10 seconds. Players vote anonymously from 1 to 5. Most points wins the crown. Results are saved to your match history.

Scalability

Our architecture is built to grow from small friend groups to massive drawing tournaments. Node.js handles thousands of concurrent connections on a single thread, Socket.io broadcasts every stroke in milliseconds, and MongoDB keeps every result safe.

Up to 8 Per Room

Rooms support 2 to 8 players. Matchmaking is instant — share a code and your friends are in within 30 seconds.

Event-Loop Backend

Node.js processes drawing events, votes, and phase changes without blocking — keeping the game smooth even with all 8 players.

Deployed & Live

Frontend on Vercel, backend on Railway. The game is live right now at asdrubal-dun.vercel.app — no setup needed.
